Here's a timeline of the creation and life of The Simpsons:

  • 1987 - The Simpsons debuts as part of the Tracy Ullman Show
  • 1989 - The Simpsons debuts as a half-hour primetime show on Fox
  • 2007 - The Simpsons Movie premieres in theatres worldwide
  • 2009 - The show celebrates its 20th anniversary
  • 2009 - The show becomes America's longest-running, primetime, scripted television series
  • 2011 - 20th Century Fox Television threatens to cancel the show, though this is later resolved and the show is renewed
  • 2014 - The Simpsons becomes available on-demand via The Simpsons World website
  • The Simpsons is currently airing its 28th season
